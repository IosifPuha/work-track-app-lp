It's simple

Let's face it: at some point we all tried some working time - tracking apps and their User Interface was not great. At all.
We all know that we don't need super fancy features or extra - geeky export formats. We just need a simple app that does the job. WorkTrack is that app.

The simple user interface will make you feel at home and get you through your workday in no time. You wont' just get use to it, you'll love it!

Easy to use

You won't get lost in all kind of buttons and features. We have just what you need.
Start your workday, pause it when you need to (you do take breaks, right?) and resume it when you're ready.

Features

Set-up your profile

You can adjust your preferences, like how much you're paid per hour, how long is your lunch break and more, we'll make sure you'll get the most accurate experience.

Smart Notifications on hands

Automatic reminders will get you back to work if you forgot to punch in after your breaks.

Know everything about the time you spent working

You can see how much you earned so far and dive into detailed statistics about your workdays and work habits in the stats section!

Why and how this app was born

Iosif Puha, Founder

"My dad often asked me to manually count the hours he worked, he would write them down on the notes app on his phone throughout the month and then he would ask me to calculate them."

Iosif Puha, Founder

"I absolutely hated the fact that I had to use a calculator to do it. I thought to myself: "There must be a better, automated way to do this!". And that's how WorkTrack was born."
